06. My bot is not trading, what’s the problem?

The bots are designed to open, manage and close trades based on specific technical indicators and algorithms; and will only do so when the underlying conditions on which it has been programed are met. If the bot is not trading, that simply means that it has not found the programed conditions to initiate trades. Every bot has an expected trades per week based on the back testing results, but they are not a guarantee of what will happen in the future

07. My bot closed a trade at a loss, why?

The bots are predominantly designed to manage risk. To protect your account, bots will occasionally, based on the programed algorithms, close a trade at a loss to prevent bigger losses
