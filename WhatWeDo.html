Backtesting

Bots are constantly being back tested to monitor any change in market conditions, and to ensure they are placed on the best brokers for their strategies. We run billions of tests per week at 14 locations to find the best candidates that we provide to our customers

Backtesting means we run the bot on previous years actuals to simulate its performance. We run these tests on a multitude of financial instruments and on several brokers to determine the best fit

Although we can’t guarantee the future performance, backtesting provides the strongest indication of the bot’s expected returns
