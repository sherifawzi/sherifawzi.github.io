DEVELOP

Algorithmic trading bots

Programing algorithmic trading bots requires trading acumen, advanced mathematical capabilities and experienced programming skills

By combining these skills SNR_ is uniquely placed to offer you a wide selection of bots, ready to be deployed

We constantly test a variety of technical indicators combinations, risk and money management methods to craft the best trading strategies for our bots

DEPLOY

Deploy bots

Setting up a successful trading bot requires several technical requirements, before deployment bots need to be backtested on a variety of broker and instrument combinations, and once deployed they need stable network connectivity and uninterrupted access to trading platforms

At SNR_ we take all that hassle away, from back testing to deployment, ensuring our bots are placed in locations as close as possible to the best brokers they perform on for quick and responsive trade execution

Backtesting

Bots are constantly being back tested to monitor any change in market conditions, and to ensure they are placed on the best brokers for their strategies. We run billions of tests per week at 14 locations to find the best candidates that we provide to our customers

Backtesting means we run the bot on previous years actuals to simulate its performance. We run these tests on a multitude of financial instruments and on several brokers to determine the best fit

Although we can’t guarantee the future performance, backtesting provides the strongest indication of the bot’s expected returns

Proximity

Bots are deployed on the servers with the closest proximity to brokers they perform best on, ensuring the quickest execution of orders, at the desired price with minimal slippage

OPTIMIZE

Optimize code

It is inevitable that all strategies decrease in performance over time when the underlying market conditions on which they have been created change

With SNR_ all deployed bots are consistently optimized to adapt to those market movements offering you peace of mind

Constant monitoring

We constantly monitor the performance of our bots, tracking their performance compared to their forecast

Adapt settings

Upon identifying that one or more factors in the algorithm have changed, we adapt the corresponding settings to return the bot to its desired performance
