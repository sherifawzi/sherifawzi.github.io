Deploy bots

Setting up a successful trading bot requires several technical requirements. Before being deployed, they need to be backtested on all available broker/instrument combinations. And when deployed, they need to have a stable network connectivity and uninterrupted access to trading platforms

At SNR_ we take all that hassle away, from back testing to deployment – making your automated trading experience seamless

Backtesting

We perform multiple back testing trials for all our bots to ensure their best possible performance

Backtesting means we run the bot on previous years actuals to simulate its performance. We run these tests on a multitude of financial instruments and on several brokers – to determine the settings that yield the highest gains and share which brokers the bots work best with

Although we can’t guarantee the exact performance in the future, backtesting though provides a strong indication on the bot’s expected returns and drawdowns
